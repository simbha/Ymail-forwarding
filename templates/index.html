Yahoo! Mail Forwarding

Hello!

Yahoo! Mail Forwarding is a free online application meant to compensate Yahoo's lack of email forwarding under their free email service.

How it works: you subscribe your email adresses and once in 15 minutes, new emails will be forwarded to your favourite email.

Subscribing

First you need to sign in, currently it supports only your Google+ account. Click here to sign in. Once you're authenticated you can continue to the Profile page, where email subscriptions can be added.
